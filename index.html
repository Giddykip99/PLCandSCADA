Simplified Cement Mill Plant With PID Control

This Project is a simplified cement production plant. The project has covered the material feeding process, the grinding process and the storage process. In the material feeding process, the operator has the ability to select which type of cement to grind. PID controllers have been used to maintain the feed rate at a specified setpoint for each raw material required. The feed rate depends on the type of cement selected.
