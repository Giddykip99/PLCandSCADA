Four-Way Traffic Light Control System

Each side of the road has traffic lights to help control the traffic movement. The cars on the Eastern and Western sides move and stop at the same time. And the cars on the Northern and Southern sides move and stops at the same time also. Timers have been used to control the traffic lights sequence.
